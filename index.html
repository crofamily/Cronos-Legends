✨ The Trinari Rise

Once ethereal whispers in the forests and winds, the Trinari now take physical form. They are not born of war, but of harmony – guardians of nature, bound to the very magic that sustains the world. Each Trinari embodies a primal element, from blazing fire sprites to shimmering crystal wisps.

But don't be fooled by their soft features – the Trinari are powerful and purposeful. Their mission is clear: restore the balance broken by war and ambition. Whether through peace… or power.
