📸 How to Add Your NFT Images

  1. Prepare your images: Save your NFT artwork as .jpg or .png files (recommended size: 400x400px or larger, square format works best)
  2. Name your files: Use descriptive names like guardian-champion.jpg, forest-sage.png, etc.
  3. Upload to your server: Place the images in the same folder as your index.html file, or create an images/ folder
  4. Update the HTML: Replace the placeholder emoji with your image:

    Change this:
    <div class="nft-image">🛡️</div>

    To this:
    <div class="nft-image"><img src="guardian-champion.jpg" alt="Guardian Champion" style="width: 100%; height: 100%; object-fit: cover;"></div>
  5. Repeat for each NFT: Update all 6 NFT cards with your actual artwork
  6. Optional: Add more NFT cards by copying the entire <div class="nft-card">...</div> section and updating the content
