🌟The Basics

What is Cronos Legends? ▼
Cronos Legends is a Web3 ecosystem built on the Cronos blockchain. It features collectible NFTs, immersive lore, and a unique games where players can compete and earn crypto rewards. The project includes multiple tokens and NFTs across three powerful factions: the Chaos Horde, Cronos Guardians, and Trinari.
What are the Factions? ▼
Cronos Legends features three powerful factions, each with unique traits and philosophy:
  • 💀 Chaos Horde – Embodies raw destruction, power, and chaos. Warriors driven by conquest and fury.
  • ⚔️ Cronos Guardians – Represents order, protection, and celestial might. Noble defenders of tradition and honor.
  • 🌿 Trinari – Mystical beings of nature embodying balance and ancient wisdom. Elemental spirits risen to restore harmony.
Your faction choice may influence your journey and available rewards throughout the Cronos Legends universe.
Is the project safe? ▼
Yes! Cronos Legends is built with transparency and community trust as core principles. All tokenomics are publicly available, the development team is active and accessible, and liquidity pools are visible on-chain for verification. We aim to stand apart from typical short-lived projects by focusing on long-term value and community engagement.
Is there a roadmap? ▼
Yes! Our roadmap outlines the project's development phases, upcoming features, and major milestones. Visit our website or Discord for the latest roadmap updates and announcements.

What is the $CLG Token? ▼
The $CLG token is the core utility and community token of Cronos Legends. It serves multiple purposes within the ecosystem:

How can I add liquidity to $CLG? ▼
Step-by-step guide to adding liquidity on VVS Finance:
  1. Visit VVS Finance and connect your wallet
  2. Navigate to the "Liquidity" section
  3. Select $CLG and pair it with $CRO or another token
  4. Make sure you select the V2 pool
  5. Enter the amount of either $CLG or $CRO/$CRONUS you want to add
  6. VVS Finance will automatically calculate the matching amount for the other token
  7. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
LP providers may be eligible for future rewards and staking benefits. Always ensure you understand impermanent loss before providing liquidity.
What is $CRONUS and how does it relate to Cronos Legends? ▼
$CRONUS is a partner project token within the Cronos Legends ecosystem. Holders of $CRONUS may receive special bonuses and benefits in Battlegrounds and other events.
